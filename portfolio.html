CCC gathered insight of leading industry competitors and conducted research to uncover recruitment strategy and assess trends in the pertaining industry to gain a finer understanding of the bottlenecks IHF encounters. Our consultants provided rigorous research to pinpoint effective ways to incorporate data analytics, a proposal of 3 new sub-channels, and competitor analysis. “What’s Up Moms” is currently the #1 parenting channel on YouTube with over 3.1 million subscribers.

CCC was tasked with creating action plans regarding social media posting strategy, promotional campaigns, and SEO best practices for Seed and Sprout. Additionally, our consultants provided recommendations regarding menu design improvements, university sponsorship opportunities, and relevant on-campus organizations to market to.

CCC provided suggestions regarding the operations of Triton Outfitters and recommended the use of productivity websites which gives teams the ability to track progress progression and hold collaborators accountable and organized. Consultants analyzed three of the most popular productivity websites and provided an in-depth overview into the features of each and how it can be used to make the organization more organized and efficient.

Dirty Birds Bar and Grill enlisted the services of Cornerstone Community Consultants to help their business with marketing.
